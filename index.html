Services

Companionship care

Companionship care is a vital service provided by Cecilia Home Care. It involves compassionate caregivers spending time with clients, engaging in meaningful conversations, and providing emotional support.

Homemaking services

Homemaking services aim to assist clients in maintaining a clean, safe, and comfortable living space. Light housekeeping tasks include dusting, vacuuming, mopping, and general tidying to keep the home orderly and neat.

Personal care

Personal care refers to the assistance provided to individuals who need help with daily activities and maintaining personal hygiene. This type of care is essential for those who may be elderly, disabled, or otherwise unable to care for themselves independently.

Live - In home care

Our live-in caregivers are skilled in delivering compassionate and professional care, ensuring clients' comfort and safety in their own homes. If you require further details on how we tailor our live-in home care services to individual needs, please let us know.

Specialized care

Our specialized care services are designed to cater to the unique requirements of each client, guaranteeing they receive the utmost care and assistance. Are you interested in learning more about how we can tailor these services for your clients?

Transportation

Transportation services are essential for ensuring that clients can attend appointments, run errands, and stay connected with their community. At Cecilia Homecare LLC, we can assist with arranging reliable transportation for our clients.

24/7 Around the clock care

Cecilia Homecare LLC recognizes the need for ongoing support and supervision for certain cients. As a result, we provide 24/7 around-the-clock care. Long-term services

Long-term services are designed to support individuals who need assistance with daily Activities over an extended period. These services are crucial for those with chronic illnesses, disabilities, or aging-related conditions.

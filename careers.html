Hourly Home health Aide

Full | Part-time (weekly pay)

Hourly home health aides are vital in providing support to individuals who require help with everyday tasks. Their typical duties include: Personal Care: Helping with bathing, dressing, grooming, and toileting. Meal Preparation: Creating, cooking, and serving meals tailored to specific dietary requirements. Medication Management: Overseeing medication schedules and ensuring timely administration. Light Housekeeping: Conducting cleaning, doing laundry, and keeping the living space orderly. Companionship: Offering social engagement and emotional support to alleviate loneliness and isolation. Mobility Assistance: Aiding in walking, moving from bed to wheelchair, and utilizing mobility devices.

  • ✔ Paid health inurance
